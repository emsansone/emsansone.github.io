About me

I am a PhD candidate at DISI (University of Trento), working on machine learning and artificial intelligence. My research focuses on understanding how to exploit unlabeled data to improve learning. Semi-supervised learning, positive-unlabeled learning and deep learning are therefore my main fields of interest.

Apart from research, I like to practice sport activities. In winter, I teach skiing in the place where I live, Fai della Paganella, and prepare specific programs for athletes and people who has the objective of becoming ski instructors.
